Polybius is treated through one concrete anchor: the internet legend of a mysterious arcade cabinet said to cause strange effects before disappearing from public view.

Polybius Arcade Game Legend: The Missing Cabinet, Rumor, and Internet Folklore works as a video because it gives the creator a clear subject, a recognizable setting, and a central motif around internet folklore, arcade game legend.
