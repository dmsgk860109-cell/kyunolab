# Kyunolab Original Archive Mode

Original Archive Mode is the Kyunolab generation mode for newly created archive stories.

This mode remains independent from Canonical Archive Mode.

Canonical Archive Mode is for existing well-known legends, myths, folklore, mysteries, and historical strange records.

Original Archive Mode is for new Kyunolab original records created after studying related folklore patterns.

## Purpose

Original Archive Mode does not mean inventing from nothing.

Before drafting a new original record, study related existing material so the article understands the larger tradition it is entering.

The research stage is for pattern analysis, not copying.

Original Mode should:

- research related legends, myths, ghost stories, folklore, mysteries, and motifs first
- identify repeated scenes, structures, settings, symbols, and emotional patterns
- avoid rewriting, translating, or lightly modifying an existing famous legend
- create a new situation, place, scene, development, or structure
- preserve Kyunolab's calm, literary, source-aware archive style

## Research Stage

Before creating a new Original Archive article, review related material from areas such as:

- urban legends
- ghost stories
- folklore
- myths
- mysteries
- internet legends
- local legends
- modern folklore
- related motifs
- similar story structures

The goal is to understand:

- which scenes repeat
- which structures are common
- which motifs appear often
- which settings are already widely known
- which endings or symbols would make the new idea feel too close to an existing legend

Do not use the research stage to collect sentences for reuse.

Do not translate existing material into a new article.

Do not take a famous legend, change a few details, and call it original.

## Original Creation Rules

After research, create a new independent Kyunolab record.

The final article should use:

- a new situation
- a new place
- a new central scene
- a new development
- a new structure or changed story logic
- a distinct emotional pressure or motif combination

The new record may be informed by folklore patterns, but it must not be a disguised rewrite of a known story.

## Similarity Guard

If research shows that the planned story is too close to a known legend, do not publish it in that form.

Rework the concept when the planned article is too similar in:

- core setup
- sequence of events
- central motif
- ending
- symbolic meaning
- famous scene

Ways to create distance:

- change the setting
- change the central object or scene
- change the story structure
- change the emotional question
- combine a familiar motif with a less expected context
- remove the detail that makes it too close to a known canonical legend

If the topic should remain close to the famous source, use Canonical Archive Mode instead.

## Kyunolab Original Philosophy

Original Archive articles are research-informed inventions.

They study the shared structure of folklore, urban legends, ghost stories, myths, and modern rumors, then create a new record from that understanding.

Search is for research, not copying.

The finished article should feel like a new file in the archive, not a borrowed legend wearing a different title.

## Metadata Notes

Set `generationMode` to:

```yaml
generationMode: original-archive
```

Original Mode draft notes should include:

- `originalResearchNotes`
- related motifs reviewed
- existing legends checked for similarity
- repeated structures observed
- changes made to protect originality

These notes are production notes. They do not need to appear as reader-facing content.

## Difference From Canonical Archive Mode

Original Archive Mode:

- researches existing legends for motif and structure
- creates a new independent record
- does not rewrite, translate, or lightly alter a known legend

Canonical Archive Mode:

- writes about an existing known legend or mystery
- does not invent a new record
- uses reliable sources to clarify variants, history, and source limits

Original Archive Mode keeps the existing Kyunolab writing style, Story & Source Note, SEO structure, category system, and output format.

This update adds only the pre-writing research stage and originality guard.
