About

Fictional stories about consequences, written for people who love a clean moral turn.

The Comeuppance Report publishes original fictional dialogue dramas. The stories are not real reports, not news, and not personal claims.

Each story is designed around a clear conflict: someone behaves badly, the situation escalates through dialogue, and the ending reveals the consequence. The format borrows the scanning rhythm of community boards and the momentum of short fiction, but every post is presented as fiction.

Kyuno Lab operates this site as a lightweight publishing experiment for English-language fictional karma stories, social sharing workflows, and automated static publishing.
