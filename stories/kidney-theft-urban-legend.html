Quick Answer

In brief, The kidney theft urban legend usually describes a traveler who is drugged, wakes in a bathtub full of ice, and discovers that one or both kidneys have been removed. The story spread widely through fax, email, and word of mouth in the 1990s. The legend is not evidence of random hotel-bar organ theft. It is a fear story about travel, bodies, medical systems, crime, and the black market.
