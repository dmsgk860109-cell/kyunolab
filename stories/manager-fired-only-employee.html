FICTIONAL CHAT DRAMA - WORKPLACE KARMA

Power-Tripping Female Boss Fired The Quiet Night-Shift Worker In The Group Chat. Then The Security Log Answered.

She wanted the whole team to watch him get humiliated. She forgot the overnight system kept better records than she did.

Published July 1, 2026 - 13 min read - Fictional messenger-style drama

Fiction notice: This is a fictional chat drama. All characters, messages, workplaces, schools, businesses, places, and events are invented for entertainment.

1. Opening Chat - The Overnight Shift

Store Team Chat: Reminder. Tonight's freezer delivery arrives between 1:10 AM and 1:40 AM.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: I have it covered. I already checked the dock camera and the vendor code.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: You always say you have it covered like you own the building.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: No. I just close alone most nights.

Lazy Senior Cashier: Can someone else do the delivery check tonight? I have plans after my shift.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: That is fine. I am scheduled until 3 AM.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Look at him volunteering again. Very heroic.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: It is on the schedule.

New Part-Time Student: I thought two people were required for freezer delivery.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Only when people are dramatic.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Company policy says two people verify frozen goods after midnight.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Company policy also says respect your manager.

2. Repeated Pattern - The Same Problem Twice

Store Team Chat: Yesterday's closing checklist is missing a signature.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Night-Shift Worker, explain.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: The signature is on page two. I uploaded it at 3:18 AM.

File History: Closing_Checklist_Tuesday.pdf uploaded by Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker at 3:18 AM.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Do not hide behind file history.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: I am not hiding. I am answering.

Lazy Senior Cashier: The freezer was clean when I left, by the way.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: It was not. I cleaned it after the melted pallet was moved.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Careful. You are starting to sound bitter.

New Part-Time Student: I saw him cleaning it when I forgot my bag.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Students see a lot of things they misunderstand.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Understood.

3. Private Pressure - Keep Quiet

Private Chat - Power-Tripping Female Boss: Stop correcting me in the team chat.

Private Chat - Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: I only corrected the checklist issue.

Private Chat - Power-Tripping Female Boss: You make me look careless.

Private Chat - Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: The records made the situation clear.

Private Chat - Power-Tripping Female Boss: You know what I mean.

Private Chat - Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: I do.

Private Chat - Power-Tripping Female Boss: Then learn. Next time I ask for an answer, give me the answer I need.

Private Chat - Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: I cannot change timestamps.

Private Chat - Power-Tripping Female Boss: Nobody asked you to change anything.

Private Chat - Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Okay.

Private Chat - Power-Tripping Female Boss: And do not quote policy at me again.

Private Chat - Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Okay.

4. Evidence Seed - The System Keeps Time

Security Log: Dock Door opened at 1:21 AM by Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker.

Security Log: Freezer Alarm acknowledged at 1:24 AM by Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker.

Vendor Driver: Your guy caught the temp issue fast. Saved me a rejected load.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Please send that to the store email.

Vendor Driver: Already did.

Store Email: Vendor note received: Overnight worker identified temperature variance before acceptance.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Boss, I rejected two damaged cartons and accepted the rest.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: You rejected product without asking?

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Policy says reject compromised frozen goods.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: There is that word again.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Policy?

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Attitude.

5. Public Line Crossed - The Group Chat Firing

Store Team Chat: Everyone read this carefully.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Effective immediately, Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker is no longer part of this team.

New Part-Time Student: Wait what?

Lazy Senior Cashier: For real?

Power-Tripping Female Boss: He repeatedly undermined management and made unauthorized decisions.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Are you terminating me in the group chat?

Power-Tripping Female Boss: I am documenting it where everyone can see why disrespect has consequences.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Please confirm I should not report tonight.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Correct. Do not enter the building.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Please confirm who will handle the 1 AM delivery and alarm check.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Anyone can do it.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Confirmed.

6. Tension Hold - Doubling Down

Store Team Chat: Who is closing tonight?

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Lazy Senior Cashier will close.

Lazy Senior Cashier: I never got dock access.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Then ask New Part-Time Student.

New Part-Time Student: I have only been here four days.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: It is a door and a thermometer.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: I am no longer authorized to advise.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Nobody asked you.

Lazy Senior Cashier: What is the alarm code for the freezer panel?

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Check the binder.

New Part-Time Student: The binder says ask Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Then the binder is outdated.

7. Authority Enters - The Owner Asks For Records

Store Owner: Why did I get a vendor escalation at 1:46 AM?

Power-Tripping Female Boss: Small misunderstanding. We had a former employee interfere with a delivery.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: I did not enter the building after termination.

Store Owner: Former employee?

New Part-Time Student: Boss fired him in this chat.

Store Owner: I can see that.

Store Owner: Who approved a group-chat termination before an overnight frozen delivery?

Power-Tripping Female Boss: I made a management decision.

Store Owner: Send me the incident report.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: I will write one now.

Store Owner: No. Send the one you used before firing him.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: There was not time.

8. Reveal - The Security Log Answers

Security Log: Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker badge disabled at 6:12 PM.

Security Log: Dock Door forced open at 1:32 AM by Lazy Senior Cashier.

Security Log: Freezer Alarm ignored from 1:35 AM to 2:08 AM.

Vendor Driver: I waited because nobody knew the verification process.

Store Owner: Why was the dock forced?

Lazy Senior Cashier: Boss said anyone could do it.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: I did not say force the door.

New Part-Time Student: You said it was just a door and a thermometer.

Store Owner: And the damaged cartons?

Vendor Driver: Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker rejected those correctly last night. I emailed proof.

Store Email: Vendor note forwarded to Store Owner.

Store Owner: So the employee you publicly fired was following policy.

9. Consequence - Official Follow-Up

Store Owner: Power-Tripping Female Boss, you are removed from scheduling and discipline authority pending review.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: You are taking his side?

Store Owner: I am taking the side of records.

HR Manager: I have the group-chat termination, private messages, and security log.

HR Manager: The termination is reversed in the system. Pay for the missed shift will be issued.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Thank you.

HR Manager: You are not required to return to overnight work unless you choose to.

Store Owner: I would like to offer a lead operations role with another supervisor.

Power-Tripping Female Boss: This is humiliating.

New Part-Time Student: So was the group chat.

Store Owner: Enough. This review continues offline.

Security Log: Freezer Alarm reset at 3:02 AM by Store Owner.

10. Aftermath - Calm Closure

Private Chat - Store Owner: I am sorry this happened publicly.

Private Chat - Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: I appreciate the apology.

Private Chat - Store Owner: Will you consider the lead role?

Private Chat - Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Only with written duties, backup coverage, and no private pressure from managers.

Private Chat - Store Owner: Agreed.

Store Team Chat: New overnight procedure is posted. Two-person verification is mandatory.

New Part-Time Student: Thank you.

Lazy Senior Cashier: Sorry for freezing up. Literally and otherwise.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: It is fine. Follow the checklist.

HR Manager: Reminder: disciplinary action is never to be announced in team chat.

Quiet Male Night-Shift Worker: Goodnight, everyone.

Security Log: Lead Operations access granted at 9:00 AM.
