Frequently Asked Questions

What are the Backrooms?

The Backrooms are a fictional internet-folklore setting: endless rooms and corridors reached by slipping outside ordinary reality in online retellings.

Where did the Backrooms originate?

The modern legend grew from an anonymous image-board post pairing an empty yellow-room photograph with a short description, after which communities expanded the setting.

Are the Backrooms real?

No verified place matches the supernatural setting. Real offices, basements, hotels, and service corridors can resemble the imagery, but the Backrooms is collaborative fiction and internet folklore.

Why do the Backrooms feel unsettling?

They combine familiar architecture with missing purpose, repeated patterns, harsh lighting, and no clear exit, producing the discomfort commonly associated with liminal spaces.
